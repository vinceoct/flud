How To Play

  • The game starts in the top left corner.
  • Clicking one of the six colors below the board will turn the starting square, and all adjacent squares of the same color, into the clicked color. Diagonal squares will remain the same.
  • To win, you must flood the board in 25 moves or less.
  • Your score is determined by the amount of moves it takes to flood the board. The lower the score the better.
  • Change the color palette by clicking the brush and selecting form the list. Doing so will reset the board
